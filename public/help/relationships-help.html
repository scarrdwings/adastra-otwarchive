The relationships in your work. Full names are preferred (for example, "Benjamin Sisko/Seven of Nine" or "William Riker & Data"). You may list more than one relationship, separated by commas. Please note that all user-created tags must be 100 characters or less; if your work contains a threesome (or a larger poly group) you may want to use last names only to avoid going over the character limit.
